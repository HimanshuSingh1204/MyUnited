About the academy

Built on the pitch, not on paper

My United Football Academy started as a weekend coaching camp and has grown into a full-time academy with age-wise programs, certified coaches, and a curriculum built around technique first, tactics second. We train players to think on the ball — not just kick it.

Technique first

Ball mastery and first touch drilled before tactics.

Age-appropriate

Separate curriculums for U-8 through adult batches.

Certified coaches

AFC/AIFF licensed coaching staff across all batches.

Match exposure

Regular friendlies and tournament participation.
